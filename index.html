Bio

I am a passionate innovator and problem solver from Botswana, sub-Saharan Africa. I am a self-taught full stack developer and AI enthusiast with research interests in Automatic Speech Recognition, Conversational AI, and Machine Learning applications for healthcare. My recent work has focused on evaluating ASR models for pathological speech, particularly for therapeutic applications involving conversational avatars [AICS'24]. My research often falls at the intersections of Speech Technology, Machine Learning, and Human-Computer Interaction.

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Cognitive Systems & Artificial Intelligence at the Technological University Dublin, Ireland. Before starting my PhD, I was the Head of Technology at Xavier Africa, where I worked as a Conversational AI architect and developed cross-platform projects for agency clients. I am also the founder of Anton Tech, which received the 2021 Botswana Innovation Fund to develop solutions that detect transboundary diseases using geospatial data. I graduated with First Class honors and as Valedictorian from both the University of Sunderland and Botswana Accountancy College in Computer Systems Engineering.

From a young age (grade 8), I developed an interest in Astronomy, computer programming and problem solving. My achievements include being the first Form 1 student in the history of the competitions to win the regional finals and participate in the national finals for my category, National Champion in the Botswana Maths & Science competitions 2013, and representing my country at the 2nd edition of the International Olympiad of Metropolises Finals 2017 held in Moscow, Russia. I am also a champion of various hackathons including the Creative Business Cup Botswana 2023, BothoHacks 2020, and multiple Botswana Innovation Hub hackathons.

My mission is to make a positive impact in my community through various community programmes and projects. My vision is to use Artificial Intelligence research to disrupt industries and solve problems for the Sub Saharan African ecosystem and the rest of the world.
